Welcome to the EVERFI Automator

Your course is now on autopilot.

Thank you for installing the bot. Access is now managed by the administrator.

  1. Navigate to an EVERFI course page (platform.everfi.net).
  2. Click the extension icon in your browser's toolbar.
  3. Click "Start Bot".

If your username is whitelisted, the bot will start. If you get an "Access Denied" error, please contact the administrator.

A quick note: The bot is not perfect and may get stuck on complex activities. If this happens, reload the page, complete that single activity manually, and the bot will take over on the next page.
